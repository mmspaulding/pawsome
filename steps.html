Getting Started

I am excited to work with you! Here are the steps to a positive pet sitting experience:

  1. Send an email or call to ask questions and begin your pet services.
  2. I will visit your home for a 30 minute, complimentary initial consultation, where we can discuss:
    • The dates of service
    • Where the pet supplies are kept
    • Your pet's walking style and special commands
    • Any special care instructions
    • How to best reach you in case of emergencies
  3. During the consulation, I will also collect a set of house keys, and the payment due.
